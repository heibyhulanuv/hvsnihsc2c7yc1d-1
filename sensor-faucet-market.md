<p><p>The sensor faucet market encompasses two primary types: infrared sensor faucets and touch button faucets. Infrared sensor faucets utilize motion detection to activate water flow, promoting hygiene by minimizing contact. These faucets are ideal for public restrooms. In contrast, touch button faucets allow users to control water flow with a simple button press, providing convenience and customizable operation. Both types prioritize water efficiency and user-friendly functionality, catering to diverse consumer needs in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
